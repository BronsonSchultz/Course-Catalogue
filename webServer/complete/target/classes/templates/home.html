Welcome!

This website is an easy look up tool when choosing classes at the U of S

Here's How to Use it:

Scheduler

The scheduler is a virtual piece of paper where you can drag and drop classes onto it to plan out your week It will help you recognize conflicts and requirements for the classes you are interested in.

My Courses

On this page you will be able to see the two lists of courses that you have checked off as completed and/or favourited.

My Degrees

Here you can see what degree programs you have favourited. This will include the requirements for each degree. Along with how much progress you have made into that degree.

Catalogue

Here is where you can search the catalogue of undergrad courses that the U of S is currently offering. The courses can be searched by subject (like CMPT), or by year level (100, 200, 300, 400).

FAQ

On the frequently asked questions page you can find answers to some questions you might have. Here you can also report any bugs you may find on the site.
